# SwingEdge — QA Audit (Desktop + Mobile · HE/EN)

**Date:** 2026-07-13
**Scope:** Read-only diagnostic audit of the live production app (`https://swing-edge.vercel.app/app`), all 9 tabs.
**Passes:** M-HE (Mobile Hebrew, 343px) · M-EN (Mobile English) · D-HE (Desktop Hebrew) · D-EN (Desktop English).
**Method:** Navigation, scrolling, tooltip/modal reads, language switching only. No trades, settings, or data were modified. Language restored to Hebrew at end.

---

## Executive Summary

~27 findings: **2 🔴 blockers, 15 🟠 medium, 10 🟡 polish.**

Two systemic themes dominate:

1. **Incomplete i18n** — English strings bleed into the Hebrew UI on almost every tab (and Hebrew bleeds back into English mode in one spot).
2. **Mobile layout** — a fixed bottom status bar overflows the viewport, the floating "+" button overlaps content on every tab, and large P&L numbers clip inside the 2-column stat grid.

Chart/data bugs (broken hold-time scatter, duplicate axis labels, blank TradingView OHLC, single-asset Market Pulse) are **viewport- and language-independent** and appear identically everywhere.

Solid and working: RTL⇄LTR flipping, calendar, forms, empty-states, and tooltips.

**Coverage caveat:** Desktop was audited fully (all 9 tabs, both languages). Mobile was audited via DevTools iPhone emulation across all 9 tabs, but on the **Analytics** tab the page could not be scrolled past the equity curve (see M-04), so the lower focus charts there were **not visually verified on mobile** — they are DOM-confirmed as present and diagnosed from desktop. Worth a quick check on a real device.

## Top 5 to Fix First

1. **L-01 — English insight-card body (🔴).** The single most prominent, most-read card in the Journal is entirely untranslated. Highest embarrassment-per-pixel; likely a missing i18n key on the AI-generated insight string.
2. **R-01 — Broken hold-time vs P&L scatter (🔴).** A headline analytics chart is non-functional (all points on one axis value). Almost certainly an axis `domain`/data-key bug — one fix restores a whole feature.
3. **M-01 — Bottom status bar overflow (🟠, every tab).** Causes a horizontal scrollbar app-wide on mobile and truncates live account stats. Add `flex-wrap`/`overflow` or hide non-essential items below `sm`. One change fixes all tabs.
4. **M-02 + M-03 — FAB overlap & clipped P&L number (🟠, every tab).** The two most visible mobile-layout defects a user hits immediately. Add a safe-area/bottom-bar offset to the FAB and shrink/auto-fit the P&L font in the stat grid.
5. **L-02/L-05/L-06/L-08 — Batch i18n cleanup of section headings (🟠).** Dozens of English headings across Dashboard, Tools, Settings, Market Intel share one root cause (hardcoded strings). Sweeping them together is high-value, low-risk, and makes the Hebrew build feel finished.
